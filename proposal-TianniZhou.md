# Preproposal

## What idea(s) do you have for your final project?

This program scrapes Delta for April 11th flights from PHL to IAH. It builds an Excel sheet with flight details, then compares options two at a time. It adds travel time as $50 per hour to the ticket price to find the best deal for that day. Besides, it will store everyday's info into the excel sheet, track the price trend, and create a graph. If the most ideal few options' price is continuing going up to a certain amount, it will send a warning in the email too. So basically, everyday's email contains a complete excel form containing all the history data and graph, price warning if needed, the best option of the day, and the Delta link if you want to check it by yourself.
